Students and Youth Development

We educate and Impact Students and Youths to have a sense of purpose in order to be relevant in their communities. We expose them to higher platforms to compete with their mates in other countries like exchange programs, debate and writing competitions, soft skills training, effective learning methods, practical hands on training and so on. There will also be health education for students and youths who will benefit from the activities and programs of the DIDACUM under this department. Anything on health shall be led by Mrs. Betty Adehoke who is a public health professional and passionate about health education for young people and children
